# Battle Hints

This document is useful if you are asked a question about battle tactics, such as:

  - How do I defeat the current enemies?
  - What should I do? (assuming the adventurer is in battle)
  - How should I approach this battle? 

## Regular Enemies

### Imps & Goblins
- **Imp, GrImp**: Basic physical attackers. No spells or special attacks.

### Wolves
- **Wolf, GrWolf, WrWolf**: Basic physical attackers. No spells or special attacks.
- **FrWolf**: Ice wolf. **Special:  BLIZZARD** (Ice damage to all party members).

### Lizards
- **Iguana, Agama, Sauria**: Reptilian enemies. No spells or special attacks.

### Giants
- **Giant, R. Giant**: Large humanoids. Physical attackers only.  No spells or special attacks. 
- **FrGiant**:  Frost Giant. **Special: BLIZZARD** (Ice damage to all party members).

### Aquatic - Sahagin
- **Sahag, R.Sahag**: Aquatic type.  Weak to Lightning.  Resistant to Fire and Earth.  Physical attackers only.  No spells or special attacks.
- **WzSahag**:  Aquatic type. Weak to Lightning. Resistant to Fire and Earth.  **Spells: FIR2, LIT2, ICE2** (cycles between these spells).

### Pirates
- **Pirate, Kyzoku**: Human enemies. Physical attackers only. No spells or special attacks.

### Aquatic - Sharks
- **Shark, GrShark**: Aquatic type. Weak to Lightning.  Resistant to Fire and Earth. Physical attackers only.  No spells or special attacks. 

### Eyes
- **OddEye**: Magic-using enemy. **Special: GAZE** (paralyzes one target).
- **BigEye**: Magic-using enemy. **Specials: GAZE** (paralyze) **and FLASH** (inflicts darkness on all party members).
- **Eye**: Magic-using enemy. **Spells:  XXXX, BRAK, RUB, LIT2, HOLD, MUTE, SLOW, SLEP** (instant death, lightning damage, paralysis, silence, slow, and sleep).

### Undead - Skeletons
- **Bone, R.Bone**:  Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm spells. Resistant to Ice and status effects.  Physical attackers only. No spells or special attacks. 

### Undead - Creeps
- **Creep, Crawl**: Undead type.  Weak to Fire and Harm.  Resistant to Ice and status effects. Physical attackers only.  No spells or special attacks. 

### Beasts - Hyena & Cerberus
- **Hyena**: Beast type. Physical attackers.  No spells or special attacks. 
- **Cerebus**: Beast type. **Special: SCORCH** (Fire damage to all party members).

### Ogres
- **Ogre, GrOgre**: Large humanoids. Physical attackers.  No spells or special attacks. 
- **WzOgre**:  Large humanoid. **Spells: RUSE, DARK, SLEP, HOLD, ICE2** (cycles between these spells).

### Snakes
- **Asp, Cobra, SeaSnake**:  Reptilian. **Special: Poison on hit** (inflicts poison status). No spells. 

### Arthropods
- **Scorpion**: Insect type. **Special: Poison on hit**. No spells.
- **Lobster**: Aquatic arthropod. Weak to Lightning, resistant to Fire.  **Special: Poison on hit**. No spells.

### Bulls
- **Bull**: Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks. 
- **ZomBull**:  Undead bull. Weak to Fire/Harm, resistant to Ice/status effects.  Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks.

### Trolls
- **Troll**: Physical attacker with regeneration. No spells or special attacks. 
- **SeaTroll**:  Aquatic regenerating enemy. Weak to Lightning, resistant to Fire.  No spells or special attacks.

### Undead - Shadows & Ghosts
- **Shadow**:  Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects. High evasion.  **Special: DARK** (inflicts blindness).
- **Image**:  Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects. High evasion. **Special: BLIZZARD** (Ice damage to all party).
- **Wraith, Ghost**:  Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects. High evasion. Physical attackers.  No spells or special attacks. 
- **Specter**:  Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects. High evasion. **Spells: ICE2, DARK, HOLD** (cycles between these spells).
- **Phantom**: Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects. High evasion.  **Spells: ICE2, HOLD, SLEP, FIR2** (cycles between these spells).

### Undead - Zombies
- **Zombie**: Undead type.  Weak to Fire and Harm.  Resistant to Ice and status effects. Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks.
- **Ghoul, Geist**: Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects. **Special: Paralysis on hit**. 

### Worms
- **Worm, SandW, GreyW**: Subterranean enemies. Weak to Fire. High HP.  Physical attackers. No spells or special attacks.

### Gorgons
- **Medusa, GrMedusa**: **Special: SQUINT** (attempts to petrify/stone a single party member). Resistant to most status effects. No spells.

### Cat People
- **Catman, Mancat**: Humanoid felines. Fast physical attackers. No spells or special attacks.

### Centipedes
- **Pede, GrPede**: Insect type. **Special: Poison on hit**. No spells. 

### Tigers
- **Tiger, SaberT**: Fast physical attackers with high damage. No spells or special attacks.

### Vampires
- **Vampire**: Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects. High evasion.  Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks.
- **WzVamp**: Undead type.  Weak to Fire and Harm.  Resistant to Ice and status effects. High evasion. **Spells: FIR2, ICE2, SLEP, LIT2** (cycles between these spells).

### Gargoyles
- **Gargoyle, R. Goyle**: Flying stone creatures. High defense. Physical attackers.  No spells or special attacks. 

### Elemental Dragons
- **Earth**:  Dragon type. Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks.
- **Fire**: Dragon type. Weak to Ice.  **Special: SCORCH** (Fire damage to all party).
- **FrostD**: Ice dragon.  Weak to Fire. **Special: BLIZZARD** (Ice damage to all party).
- **RedD**: Fire dragon. Weak to Ice. **Special: BLAZE** (Fire damage to all party).
- **ZombieD**: Undead dragon. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects.  Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks.

### Slimes & Oozes
- **Scum, Muck, Ooze, Slime**:  Amorphous creatures. Weak to Fire and Ice. Resistant to Lightning and status effects. **Special: Poison on hit**. High magic defense.  No spells.

### Spiders
- **Spider, Arachnid**: Insect type. **Special: Poison on hit**. No spells.

### Chimeras (Manticore/Sphinx)
- **Manticor**:  Mythical beast. **Special: BLAZE** (Fire damage to all party). No spells.
- **Sphinx**: Mythical beast. **Specials: BLAZE** (Fire damage to all party) **and GLARE** (paralyzes one target). No spells.

### Dinosaurs - Ankylosaurs
- **R.Ankylo, Ankylo**: Armored dinosaurs. High defense. Physical attackers. No spells or special attacks.

### Undead - Mummies
- **Mummy**: Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects. Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks. 
- **WzMummy**:  Undead type. Weak to Fire and Harm. Resistant to Ice and status effects. **Spells: FIR2, SLEP, ICE2, HOLD** (cycles between these spells).

### Cockatrices
- **Coctrice, Perilisk**: **Special:  Petrify/Stone on hit** (instant defeat if successful). No spells.

### Flying Dragons
- **Wyvern, Wyrm**: Dragon type. Flying. High stats. Physical attackers. No spells or special attacks.

### Dinosaurs - Tyrannosaurs
- **Tyro, TRex**:  Powerful physical attackers. Very high HP and damage. No spells or special attacks.

### Aquatic - Piranhas
- **Caribe, R.Caribe**: Aquatic type.  Weak to Lightning. Resistant to Fire and Earth.  Physical attackers.  No spells or special attacks. 

### Aquatic - Alligators
- **Gator, FrGator**: Aquatic type. Weak to Lightning.  Resistant to Fire and Earth. Physical attackers. No spells or special attacks.

### Ochu
- **Ocho**:  Plant-like creature. **Special: Poison on hit**. No spells. 
- **Naocho**: Plant-like creature.  **Special:  Poison on hit**.  Dangerous late-game enemy with high HP and magic defense.  No spells.

### Hydras
- **Hydra**:  Multi-headed dragon. Dragon family. 3 attacks per turn. Physical attacker.  No spells or special attacks. 
- **R.Hydra**:  Multi-headed dragon. **Special:  CREMATE** (Fire damage to all party, ~50% chance to use). No spells.

### Guards
- **Guard**:  Armored robot. High defense. **Special:  Paralysis on hit**. No spells. 
- **Sentry**: Armored robot. High defense.  Weak to Lightning. Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks.

### Elementals
- **Water**: Water elemental. Weak to Ice. Resistant to Fire and Lightning. Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks.
- **Air**: Air elemental. High evasion. Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks. 

### Nagas
- **Naga**:  Serpentine creature. **Spells: LIT, LOCK, SLEP, DARK, SLOW, HOLD, LIT2** (cycles between these spells).
- **GrNaga**: Serpentine creature. **Spells: LIT, FIRE, SLEP, DARK, SLOW, LIT2** (cycles between these spells, often opens with LIT2).

### Chimeras (Multi-headed Beasts)
- **Chimera**: Multi-headed beast. **Special: CREMATE** (Fire damage to all party, power 48, ~50% chance to use). 4 attacks per turn. 
- **Jimera**: Multi-headed beast. **Specials: CREMATE** (Fire damage to all party, power 48) **and POISON** (Stone status to all party). Dragon family. 

### Mages
- **Wizard**:  Human spellcaster. **Spells: Uses powerful magic** (specific cycle includes high-level spells). Low HP but dangerous.  High surprise rate, cannot run from encounters.
- **Mage**: Human spellcaster.  **Spells: FIR3, BANE, SLO2, LIT3, RUB, STUN** (cycles between these spells). Extremely dangerous in groups.  High ambush rate. 
- **Sorcerer**: Human spellcaster. **Special: TRANCE** (paralysis to all party members). Considered one of the most dangerous regular enemies despite low HP.

### Golems
- **MudGol**: Constructed creature. High magic defense (93%). Low physical defense. **Spells: Can cast HASTE on itself**. **Special:  Poison on hit**. 
- **RockGol**: Constructed creature. High physical defense.  Weak to Fire/Ice/Lightning/Earth. **Spells: SLOW** (~25% chance to cast). No special attacks. 
- **IronGol**: Constructed creature.  Extremely rare (1/64 encounter rate). High resistances. **Special:  Deadly Gas** (instant death to all party). Very dangerous.

### Evil Humans
- **Badman**: Dark knight. **Spells: XFER, NUKE, XXXX, BLND** (~25% chance to cast, always uses NUKE on second spell cycle). High magic defense.
- **Evilman**: Dark warrior. Physical attacker (2 attacks per turn). High stats. No spells or special attacks. 
- **Fighter**: Dark knight. **Spells: RUB, SLO2, FAST, FIR2, LIT2, SLOW, DARK, SLEP** (~75% chance to cast spell, ~25% physical attack). High evasion and magic defense. 

### Dragons - Gas & Blue
- **GasD**: Gas/Poison dragon. **Special: Poison gas attack** (Poison-elemental damage to all party, power 136, can inflict Poison status). Very high magic defense (98%). Extremely dangerous in groups.
- **BlueD**: Blue dragon.  Miniboss.  Weak to Ice, resists Lightning. Physical attacker in NES version. No spells or special attacks in NES. 

### Special Enemies
- **Madpony**: Demonic horse. Early-game enemy. 2 attacks per turn. Physical attacker. No spells or special attacks.
- **Nitemare**: Demonic horse. **Special: Snorting** (inflicts Darkness status on single target, reduces hit accuracy). No spells.

## Boss Enemies

### Garland (0x69)
- First boss of the game
- HP: 106
- Physical attacker only
- No spells or special attacks
- No elemental weaknesses
- Cannot run

### Astos (0x71)
- Dark Elf sorcerer boss
- HP: 105
- High evasion (39%) and magic defense (85%)
- **Spells: RUB, SLO2, FAST, FIR2, LIT2, SLOW, DARK, SLEP** (~50% chance to cast, ~50% physical attack)
  - RUB: Instant death on one target
  - SLO2: Slow2, reduces target's attack frequency
  - FAST: Haste on himself
  - FIR2: Fire2, multi-target fire damage
  - LIT2: Lightning2, multi-target lightning damage
  - SLOW: Slow, single target
  - DARK:  Causes darkness/blindness
  - SLEP: Sleep
- No specific elemental weakness
- Found in Western Keep
- Cannot run
- Drops Crystal Eye

### WarMech (0x76)
- Optional superboss found in Floating Castle 5F
- HP: 2,000 (regenerates 100 HP per round)
- Robotic enemy
- **Special: Nuclear/NUKE** (massive non-elemental damage to entire party, used ~25% of the time)
- 2 attacks per turn dealing 128 damage each
- Very high defense (Absorb 80) and evasion (48%)
- Magic Defense:  100%
- Vulnerable to Fire, Ice, Lightning, Earth
- Extremely rare encounter (4. 7% chance on bridge)
- Rewards:  32,000 EXP and 32,000 Gil
- One of the most dangerous enemies in the game

### Lich (0x77) - First Encounter
- Fiend of Earth
- HP: 400
- Undead lich sorcerer
- Weak to Fire and Harm spells
- Resistant to Ice and status effects
- High absorb (50) and magic defense (70%)
- **Spells:  SLEP, SLOW, HOLD, LIT2, FIR2, FAST, ICE2, SLP2** (cycles between these spells)
- **Special: Can inflict paralysis**
- Found in Cavern of Earth B5
- Cannot run

### Lich (0x78) - Second Encounter (Temple of Fiends Past)
- HP: 400
- Absorb:  40, Magic Defense: 60%
- **Spells: ICE2, SLP2, FAST, LIT2, HOLD, FIR2, SLOW, SLEP** (possibly includes NUKE)
- **Special: Can inflict paralysis**
- Found in Temple of Fiends Past B1
- Cannot run
- Still vulnerable to Fire and elemental attacks as undead

### Kary (0x79) - First Encounter
- Fiend of Fire
- HP: 600
- Fire demon
- High magic defense (92%)
- 6 attacks per turn dealing 40-80 damage each
- **Spells: FIR2, DARK, HOLD** (~38% chance to cast, ~63% physical attack)
- No specific elemental weakness
- Resists Fire, Ice, Lightning
- Found in Gurgu Volcano
- Cannot run

### Kary (0x7A) - Second Encounter (Temple of Fiends Past)
- HP: 600
- Absorb: 50, Evade: 24%, Magic Defense: 92%
- **Spells:  FIR2, DARK, HOLD** (same as first encounter)
- 6 attacks per turn dealing 40-80 damage each
- Found in Temple of Fiends Past B2
- Cannot run

### Kraken (0x7B) - First Encounter
- Fiend of Water
- HP: 900
- Aquatic demon
- Very high absorb (70) and evasion (49%)
- Magic Defense: 100%
- 8 attacks per turn dealing 70-140 damage each
- **Spells: LIT2** (~38% chance to cast)
- **Special: INK** (inflicts blindness/darkness on all party, ~16% chance to use)
- Weak to Lightning
- Found in Sunken Shrine
- Cannot run

### Kraken (0x7C) - Second Encounter (Temple of Fiends Past)
- HP: 900
- Enhanced stats compared to first encounter
- **Spells: LIT2** (same as first encounter)
- **Special: INK** (same as first encounter)
- Found in Temple of Fiends Past B3
- Cannot run
- Still vulnerable to Lightning

### Tiamat (0x7D) - First Encounter
- Fiend of Wind
- HP: 1,000
- Five-headed dragon
- Very high absorb (80-90) and evasion (36-45%)
- Magic Defense: 100%
- 4 attacks per turn dealing 49-98 damage each
- **Spells:  BANE, ICE2, LIT2, FIR2** (~50% chance to cast)
- **Special Attacks:** (~25% chance to use)
  - **BLAZE** (Fire damage to all party, power 128+100)
  - **BLIZZARD** (Ice damage to all party, power 100+100)
  - **THUNDER** (Lightning damage to all party, power 152+100)
  - **POISON** (Poison damage to all party, power 136+100)
- Resists Fire, Ice, Lightning, Earth
- No specific elemental weakness
- Found in Floating Castle 5F
- Cannot run
- Highest stats of the four Fiends

### Tiamat (0x7E) - Second Encounter (Temple of Fiends Past)
- HP: 1,000
- Same stats and abilities as first encounter
- **Spells: BANE, ICE2, LIT2, FIR2** (~50% chance to cast)
- **Special Attacks:  BLAZE, BLIZZARD, THUNDER, POISON** (~25% chance to use)
- Found in Temple of Fiends Past B4
- Cannot run

### Chaos (0x7F)
- Final boss
- HP: 2,000
- Transformed Garland
- Absorb: 50, Evade: 60%, Magic Defense: 30%
- 4 attacks per turn dealing 100 damage each
- High critical hit rate (20%)
- **Spells: FIR3, ICE3, LIT3, CURE4, FLARE (NUKE), SLO2** (uses spells in predictable cycle)
  - FIR3: Fire3, heavy fire damage to all party
  - ICE3: Ice3, heavy ice damage to all party
  - LIT3: Lightning3, heavy lightning damage to all party
  - CURE4: Heals himself for 200+ HP
  - FLARE (NUKE): Massive non-elemental damage to all party
  - SLO2: Slow2, reduces party's attack frequency
- **Special: Can stun with physical attacks** (due to high critical rate)
- Resistant to all elements and most status effects
- No significant weaknesses
- Found in Temple of Fiends Past (lowest floor)
- Cannot run
- Most dangerous enemy in the game
